Who can see your friends list?

Remember that your friends control who can see their friendships on their own timelines. If people
can see your friendship on another timeline, they'll be able to see it in News Feed, search and
other places on Facebook. If you set this to Only me, only you will be able to see your full friends
list on your timeline. Other people will only see mutual friends.
